Welcome to CheetahWatch

Statistics for the Huawei E220/E270 USB HSDPA modem.

  • Signal Strength, Network and Mode, signal and mode are shown in the application window and in the status area at the top of your main screen, and network is shown in the menu.
  • Connection statistics, showing you the amount of data you've transferred in this session, and current throughput.
  • Connection control, connect and disconnect your HSDPA connection without the Modem menu.
  • Usage history, keep track of how much data you are using and reset anytime you like.
    This does have some privacy implications - a record of each connection is stored in a database file in your Library folder. You can disable this at any time in the Usage History menu.
  • Update checking, conveniently located in the CheetahWatch menu and automatic checks to keep your copy of CheetahWatch up to date.
